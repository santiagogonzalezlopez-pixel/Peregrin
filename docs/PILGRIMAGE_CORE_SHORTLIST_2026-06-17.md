# Peregrin pilgrimage core shortlist

Date: 2026-06-17

## Editorial rule

Peregrin is not a catalogue of every important Catholic church.

For a place to enter Peregrin, it should have a clear pilgrimage reason:

- Tomb or body/relic of a saint.
- Relic of Christ or the Passion.
- Recognized or deeply rooted Marian devotion.
- Living national or international pilgrimage.
- Martyr memory.
- Jubilee / indulgenced pilgrim route.
- Strong Catholic tradition where pilgrims actually go with intention.

Beautiful cathedrals without a clear pilgrim reason should wait.

This shortlist is intentionally capped. The next content phase should add these
45 places, and then stop unless we deliberately replace one.

## Selected next additions: 45

### Rome and Italy: 10

1. Basilica of Saint John Lateran, Rome, Italy
   - Reason: Papal basilica, cathedral of the Bishop of Rome, Holy Door.
   - Peregrin value: core Jubilee / Rome pilgrimage.

2. Basilica of Saint Mary Major, Rome, Italy
   - Reason: Papal basilica, major Marian basilica, Holy Door.
   - Peregrin value: core Jubilee / Marian Rome pilgrimage.

3. Basilica of Saint Paul Outside the Walls, Rome, Italy
   - Reason: Papal basilica, tomb of Saint Paul, Holy Door.
   - Peregrin value: apostolic tomb pilgrimage.

4. Basilica of the Holy Cross in Jerusalem, Rome, Italy
   - Reason: Passion relics; one of the traditional Seven Pilgrim Churches.
   - Peregrin value: relic pilgrimage.

5. Basilica of Saint Lawrence Outside the Walls, Rome, Italy
   - Reason: traditional Seven Pilgrim Churches route; martyr memory.
   - Peregrin value: Rome pilgrim route.

6. Basilica of Saint Sebastian Outside the Walls, Rome, Italy
   - Reason: traditional Seven Pilgrim Churches route; catacomb/martyr memory.
   - Peregrin value: Rome pilgrim route.

7. Pontifical Sanctuary of the Holy House of Loreto, Loreto, Italy
   - Reason: Holy House tradition; international Marian shrine.
   - Peregrin value: one of the great Marian pilgrim places.

8. Basilica of Saint Anthony of Padua, Padua, Italy
   - Reason: tomb and relics of Saint Anthony; international shrine.
   - Peregrin value: saint/relic pilgrimage.

9. Shrine of Our Lady of the Rosary of Pompei, Pompei, Italy
   - Reason: major Rosary devotion and pontifical Marian shrine.
   - Peregrin value: Marian pilgrimage and prayer tradition.

10. Basilica of Saint Mary of the Angels / Porziuncola, Assisi, Italy
    - Reason: Porziuncola, Saint Francis, Franciscan pilgrimage and indulgence.
    - Peregrin value: Franciscan memory beyond the existing Assisi stop.

### France: 6

11. Notre-Dame de Paris, Paris, France
    - Reason: veneration of the Crown of Thorns.
    - Peregrin value: Passion relic and Marian cathedral pilgrimage.

12. Sanctuary of Saint Therese of Lisieux, Lisieux, France
    - Reason: Saint Therese, Carmel and basilica.
    - Peregrin value: saint pilgrimage, strong popular devotion.

13. Sanctuary of Our Lady of La Salette, La Salette-Fallavaux, France
    - Reason: Marian apparition recognized by the local bishop.
    - Peregrin value: mountain Marian pilgrimage.

14. Sanctuary of the Sacred Heart, Paray-le-Monial, France
    - Reason: apparitions of Jesus to Saint Margaret Mary Alacoque.
    - Peregrin value: Sacred Heart pilgrimage.

15. Sanctuary of Rocamadour, Rocamadour, France
    - Reason: Black Madonna and ancient pilgrim way.
    - Peregrin value: historic Marian pilgrimage.

16. Shrine of Saint John Vianney, Ars-sur-Formans, France
    - Reason: Saint John Vianney and his body/tomb.
    - Peregrin value: saint pilgrimage and priestly spirituality.

### Europe and Eastern Mediterranean: 8

17. Catholic National Shrine of Our Lady of Walsingham, Walsingham, England
    - Reason: historic English Marian shrine and national Catholic pilgrimage.
    - Peregrin value: "England's Nazareth".

18. Einsiedeln Abbey, Einsiedeln, Switzerland
    - Reason: Black Madonna and Switzerland's major pilgrimage place.
    - Peregrin value: Marian/monastic pilgrimage.

19. Church of Our Lady Victorious / Infant Jesus of Prague, Prague, Czechia
    - Reason: worldwide devotion to the Infant Jesus of Prague.
    - Peregrin value: Christ-child devotion.

20. Gate of Dawn Chapel, Vilnius, Lithuania
    - Reason: miraculous Marian image and major Lithuanian pilgrimage site.
    - Peregrin value: Marian icon pilgrimage.

21. Basilica of the Assumption, Aglona, Latvia
    - Reason: major Latvian Catholic shrine; Assumption pilgrimage.
    - Peregrin value: Baltic Catholic anchor.

22. House of the Virgin Mary, Ephesus, Turkiye
    - Reason: ancient Christian tradition connected to Mary and Saint John.
    - Peregrin value: Marian/apostolic pilgrimage.

23. Shrine of Our Lady of Lebanon, Harissa, Lebanon
    - Reason: national Marian shrine and living pilgrimage place.
    - Peregrin value: Middle Eastern Marian anchor.

24. Sanctuary of Saint Charbel, Annaya, Lebanon
    - Reason: tomb of Saint Charbel; major Maronite pilgrimage.
    - Peregrin value: saint/tomb pilgrimage.

## Implementation order

Do not add all 45 in one risky release unless there is time for a full review.

Recommended chunks:

1. Rome and Italy pack: 10 places, plus the route "Seven Pilgrim Churches of Rome".
2. France and Europe pack: 14 places.
3. Americas and Caribbean pack: 10 places.
4. Asia and Africa pack: 11 places.

The Rome pack is the strongest next update because it gives Peregrin a very
coherent pilgrimage route rather than just more dots on the map.

## Parking lot, not selected now

Good places, but not for this first locked layer:

- Mont-Saint-Michel, France: beautiful and historic pilgrimage, but France is
  already getting six stronger saint/relic/apparition places.
- Marija Bistrica, Croatia: strong national Marian shrine, but Croatia already
  has Trsat and broader coverage can wait.
- Milan Cathedral, Italy: magnificent, but less "go with your backpack for a
  saint/relic/devotion" than the selected Italy list.
- Florence Cathedral, Italy: magnificent, but more architectural than pilgrim.
- Westminster Cathedral, England: important, but Walsingham is the true pilgrim
  anchor.
- Saint John's Co-Cathedral, Malta: important and beautiful, but not as urgent
  as the selected living pilgrimage places.
