# Catholic atlas content audit

Date: 2026-06-17

## Current app inventory

Peregrin currently contains:

- 35 countries
- 163 sanctuaries
- 15 routes
- 25 achievements

The automated data audit reports no duplicate IDs, no missing translations, no
route stop issues, no achievement issues and no certificate rule issues.

Strongest coverage today:

- Spain: 20 sanctuaries
- Philippines: 18 sanctuaries
- Holy Land: 10 sanctuaries
- Belgium: 7 sanctuaries
- Portugal: 6 sanctuaries
- France, Germany, Italy, Poland, Ireland, Canada, United States: 5-7 each

Thin coverage today:

- Africa is present but still thin: several countries have only 1 sanctuary.
- Oceania is present and coherent, but deliberately minimal.
- Latin America has the big anchors, but several strongly Catholic countries are
  still absent.
- Europe has the classic Western anchors, but major Catholic countries and
  pilgrimage places are still missing.

## Reality check

We are not 10 sanctuaries away from a complete-feeling Catholic atlas.

For a polished production app, the current content is already respectable. For a
"serious worldwide Catholic atlas", the missing layer is bigger:

- Minimum next serious pass: about 35-45 additions.
- Strong curated world atlas: about 80-120 additions over time.
- "All important Catholic cathedrals and shrines": hundreds, not recommended.

Why: GCatholic lists 4 major basilicas and 1,944 minor basilicas worldwide, plus
very large cathedral counts by country: Italy alone has 226 cathedrals, Spain 74,
France 100, Brazil 278, the USA 194 and India 173. GCatholic also lists 19
international shrines and 261 national shrines. Trying to include everything
would turn Peregrin into a database project instead of a beautiful pilgrim
passport.

### Tier 2: expansion after the must-haves

These would make the app feel much fuller without becoming unmanageable:

- More Spanish and Italian cathedrals only when they add pilgrim value, not just
  because they are architecturally famous.
- More national shrines in the Americas.
- A Baltic / Central Europe pack.
- A Southeast Asia Catholic pack.
- A "major Marian shrines of the world" route.
- A "Rome and Jubilee churches" route.
- A "Holy Family / early Christianity beyond the Holy Land" route, only if the
  Catholic relevance is clean.

## Recommended next content roadmap

Do not add 100 at once. The safest path is themed packs:

1. Rome and the Universal Church: 8-12 places.
2. European Catholic landmarks: 20-25 places.
3. Latin America national shrines: 18-25 places.
4. Asia Catholic memory: 12-18 places.
5. Africa second pass: 10-15 places.

That would bring Peregrin from 163 to roughly 230-255 sanctuaries, which is a
very strong curated world atlas without making the app feel bloated.

## Product judgement

The app should not aim to include every cathedral. It should aim to include the
places a Catholic pilgrim recognizes, dreams of visiting, or would expect in a
beautiful pilgrimage passport.

Best target:

- Curated, beautiful, spiritually meaningful.
- Enough breadth that the world map feels alive.
- Enough restraint that each place still feels chosen.
